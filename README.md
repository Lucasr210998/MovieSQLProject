# MovieSQLProject
A Select of querys I wrote to gather information from the data

Within this repo you will find the querys I wrote to gather specific information from the data, the data itself was obtained from https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/rounakbanik/the-movies-dataset. Their is also a python query I wrote as I had to adjust the format of some of the colums to convert them to the correct JSON format so I could parse through them.

Query1 Highest Grossing Actor:
Which actor had the highest total box office revenue across all movies.

Query2 Highest Grossing ROI:
Which actor appears in movies with the highest aggregate ROI = (revenue−budget)/budget (only where budget>0)?

Query3 Top Actor Under 10:
Which actor had the higherst average ROI with under 10 movies.

Query4 Highest grossing director:
Which director has the highest total revenue across their directed films?

Query5 Actor director with most revenue
Which actor–director pair generates the highest total revenue (sum revenue of movies where actor appears AND director directed)?

Query6 Actor and director ratings:
Which actor–director pair yields the highest user rating, with minimum 50 ratings?

Query7 Most frequent actor director collab:
Which actor and director have collaborated the most times?

Query8 Breakout actor:
For each director, which actor appears most frequently in their films?

Query10 Genre specific actors:
Which actors dominate specific genres (e.g., most movies in Action) and how does that correlate with revenue?

Query11 Top 3 actors per decade by revenue:
The top 3 actors of each decade that generated the most revenue.

Query13 Top 10 movies per year by revenue:
The 10 most profitable movies of each decade handling ties as well.

Query15 High revenue and high rating:
Among movies with avg user rating ≥ 4.0 and at least 100 ratings, which generated the most revenue?

Query17 Underrated successful movies:
Which movies had a low rating but ended up generating a lot of revenue.

Query19 Highest grossing genre:
Which genres are associated with the highest revenue generating movies.

Query21 Genre trends over time:
How does revenue share by genre change per decade?

Query25 Language vs revenue:
Which original languages have the highest average revenue and how does that change by decade?

Query28 Missing coverage:
What percentage of movies have credits, taglines, and ratings coverage (all three)?

Quert32 Keyword specific revenue:
What is the average revenue of movies that contain a specific keyword or phrase?
